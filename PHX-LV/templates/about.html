Honeywell:
Improving Flight Plans with Big Data and AI/ML

1. The Problem

Flight-planning is a multi-faceted task that spans across multiple domains, including those of logistics, finance, physics, mathematics, and academia.

2. Data Collection Issues

Due to the competitive nature of the commercial flight industry, it is paramount that data be kept secret from other flight companies. Access to pivotal flight often requires signing a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) with a certain company. Due to our inability to obtain NDAs from Honeywell, our group had to look elsewhere for flight data. The options on the open source Internet are slim, but ended up enabling us to at least improve the ETA metric of flight-planning.
